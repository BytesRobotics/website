Garbage Bytes - The System

Garbage Bytes encompasses far more than just your simple trash can. From trash recognition, autonomous navigation, and emotional response, the Garbage Bytes ecosystem is a revolutionary way to add interactive robots to a campus.

Autonomous Navigation

easily controllable

Each Garbage Byte is equipped with a NVIDIA Jetson TX2, GPS, ultrasonic sensors, and a navigation camera to autonomously navigate around a campus. Additionally, the whole ecosystem is easily controllable from the web interface.

Trash Recognition

custom neural network

A custom neural network architecture recognizes trash and will navigate to the nearest user to encourage them to pick up the trash. Statistics are recorded on the amount of trash, type of trash, and other data to anaylize the effectiveness of trash cleanup campaigns.

Emotional Intelligence

Unique personalities

Each Garbage Byte has an LCD face along with a speaker to interact with people around it. Every Garbage Byte has a unique personality and facial expressions, and will react to cues such as speech, weather, and motion. This user interaction makes the system friendly.
